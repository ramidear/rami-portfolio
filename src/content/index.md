  <p>
    For these projects, I managed the full lifecycle of content creation:
  </p>
  <ul style="padding-left: 1.2rem;">
    <li><strong>Concept:</strong> Scripting and storyboarding to align with current trends and organizational goals.</li>
    <li><strong>Capture:</strong> Filming on-site at events (e.g., 4th of July Festival, Student Workshops).</li>
    <li><strong>Post-Production:</strong> Editing in <strong>Canva</strong> and <strong>Adobe CC</strong> to add branding, captions, and audio.</li>
    <li><strong>Distribution:</strong> Scheduling via <strong>Meta Business Suite</strong> to optimize reach based on follower activity times.</li>
  </ul>
